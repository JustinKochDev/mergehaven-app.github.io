Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Justin Koch ("we", "us", or "our") built Mergehaven - Merge Puzzle ("the App") as a free-to-play mobile game with optional in-app purchases. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We strive to minimize data collection. Here is what we collect and why:

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use certain data to show relevant ads, including:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA), subject to your App Tracking Transparency choice
  • General device information (device model, OS version)
  • IP address (used to estimate general location at city or region level)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings or by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt when first launching the App. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchase Data

In-app purchases (gem packs, energy refills, and subscriptions) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Apple handles all transaction processing according to their own privacy policy.

Game Data

Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your gameplay data on any external servers.

Analytics

We do not use any first-party analytics services. Basic crash reporting may be provided through Apple's built-in mechanisms if you have opted in to share analytics with app developers in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared with the following third-party services solely for the purposes described above:

  • Google AdMob — for serving advertisements
  • Apple — for processing in-app purchases and optional crash reporting

Data Retention

Since game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. We do not retain any personal data on external servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Since we store minimal data and all game data resides on your device, most of these rights are automatically satisfied. For any privacy-related requests, please contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
