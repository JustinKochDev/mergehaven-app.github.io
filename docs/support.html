Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge items?

Drag and drop two identical items on the board to combine them into a higher-tier item. You can also tap a source to spawn new items when you have enough energy.

How does energy work?

Energy is used to spawn new items from sources. It regenerates automatically over time (1 energy every 3 minutes). You can also watch a rewarded video ad for bonus energy, purchase an Energy Refill from the shop, or subscribe to VIP Pass for faster regeneration and a higher energy cap.

What is the VIP Pass?

The VIP Pass is a monthly subscription ($4.99/month) that gives you faster energy regeneration, a higher energy cap, ad-free gameplay, and bonus rewards. You can subscribe or cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the Shop in the game and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore your VIP Pass subscription status. Note that consumable purchases (gems, energy refills) cannot be restored as they are consumed immediately.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Find Mergehaven and tap Cancel Subscription. Your VIP benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

My game progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you've deleted and reinstalled the app, progress may be lost. We recommend keeping regular iCloud backups of your device to protect your save data.

I'm having trouble with an in-app purchase.

If a purchase didn't go through or you were charged but didn't receive your items, try restarting the app first. If the issue persists, contact us at the email below with your approximate purchase date and we'll help resolve it. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support directly.
