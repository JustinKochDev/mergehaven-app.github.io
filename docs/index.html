Core Gameplay

Intuitive Merge Mechanics

Drag and drop matching items on the board to create higher-tier creations. Clear obstacles with tools, manage your energy wisely, and complete chapter objectives to progress through the story.

  • 5 item types: Flowers, Tools, Stones, Gems, Discoveries
  • 8+ tiers per type to unlock and collect
  • Critical merges for mastery-boosted upgrades
  • Multiple obstacle types: weeds, rocks, fog
